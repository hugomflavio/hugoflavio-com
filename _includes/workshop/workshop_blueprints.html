20mm respirometer cap

The main limitation of such a small respirometer is ensuring that the cap openings are wide enough to allow enough water flow without compromising the recirculation pump. At these small diameters, physics and drag really start to play against you. Keeping openings wide enough is a challenge because we have very little space to work with. However, with some clever sheet stacking tricks, we can increase our working space considerably.
